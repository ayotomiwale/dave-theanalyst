Unveiling Udemy's Educational Landscape

Embarking on the Udemy Data Analytics Project, I sought to reimagine the exploration of course data initially handled with Excel and PowerBI by a fellow Udemy data team member. Leveraging Python and PowerBI, my focus narrowed to four pivotal course categories: Business Finance, Graphics Design, Musical Instruments, and Web Development. The project's core objectives included scrutinizing the course landscape within each subject area, assessing subscriber counts, evaluating course ratings, and delving into revenue generation. Beyond these metrics, the endeavor extended to identifying keywords associated with top-rated and highly sought-after courses in each category.
